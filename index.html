GuessIt

Rules :

  • Number of Questions: The game consists of 10 questions that you must answer.
  • Database-Derived Questions: All questions are generated from a static database, ensuring a unique and diverse set of challenges.
  • Answer Selection: Each question will present 4 possible answers, but it's important to note that the correct answer may not always be among the randomly selected choices.
  • Scoring System: You'll be awarded 1 point if your guess is either the correct answer or the closest guess to the correct answer.
  • Restart Button: At any time during the game, you can restart by clicking on the "Restart" button. This resets the game.
  • Have Fun: Enjoy the challenge and have fun testing your skills with questions derived from the database. Your ability to make educated guesses is key to mastering this game!
